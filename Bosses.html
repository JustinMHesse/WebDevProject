Bosses of Gielenor

Slaying bosses is one of the fundamental parts of Runescape. Defeating them can reward the player with improved gear, and one of the most important things in Runescape; money! Called GP for gold-pieces, runescape players can never have enough money. GP is used to buy items from NPC vendors or from other players at the Grand Exchange. Bosses can drop expensive pieces of gear or ability upgrades after being defeated which players can either choose to keep for themselves and use, or sell to another player for GP. Defeating bosses is one of the best ways to make money in Gielenor!

Raksha, the Shadow Colossus

Raksha was a normal dinosaur that had lived on Gielenor long ago. The creature stumbled upon a powerful elder artefact that imbued him with immense power and turned him intelligent and savage. His power relied on a dark resource called shadow anima which had not been produced in ages, and as a result, Raksha slipped into a coma for tens of thousands of years until the World Guardian discovered and slayed him.

Rare drops from Raksha

  • Laceration Boots
  • Blast Diffusion Boots
  • Fleeting Boots
  • Greater Ricochet Ability Codex
  • Greater Chain Ability Codex
  • Divert Ability Codex

Zamorak, Lord of Chaos

Zamorak is a powerful god who has existed in Runescape lore since the beginning of time. After a war between the Elder Gods, Zamorak gained newfound powers and has taken refuge on Gielenor, where he has been opening portals to allow demons to infiltrate the world in hopes of subduing the human population. With help from another god, Guthix, the World Guardian is able to take on Zamorak and stop his plan.

Rare Drops from Zamorak

  • Codex of Lost Knowledge
  • Chaos Roar Ability Codex
  • Vestments of Havoc Hood
  • Vestments of Havoc Robe Top
  • Vestments of Havoc Robe Bottom
  • Vestments of Havoc Boots
  • Bow of the Last Guardian

Solak, the Grove Guardian

Solak is an ancient creature that defends a sacred area called the Lost Grove from those who seek to take its power. He was made into a powerful Guardian by the god Guthix. An elite elf warrior named Erethdor learned of the powers of the Lost Grove and attacked Solak in order to claim it for himself. He was defeated, but as his body was destroyed his mind split into two; one half of which was bound within Solak, corrupting the Guardian. The World Guardian discovered this tainted Solak, and was able to defeat him and expel Erethdor from his mind.

Rare Drops from Solak

  • Blightbound Crossbow
  • Off-hand Blightbound Crossbow
  • Erethdor's Grimoire
  • Ancient Elven Ritual Shard
  • Cinderbane Gloves

Kerapac, the Bound

Kerapac is a dragonkin being who was tasked with reclaiming the Elder God eggs, which are powerful artefacts capable of ending the current universe of Runescape in order to create a new one. He was sent with an army of soldiers to accomplish this task and retrieve the Elder God eggs from the city of Senntisten. He wields the powerful Staff of Armadyl and is a servant to the Elder God Jas.

Rare Drops from Kerapac

  • Kerapac's Wrist Wraps
  • Greater Concentrated Blast Ability Codex
  • Scripture of Jas
  • Fractured Staff of Armadyl
