The World of Runescape

Runescape is a high-fantasy massively multiplayer online role playing game created by Jagex in 2001. The game has maintained a steady playerbase and is still enjoyed by veteran and new players to this day! It was originally a browser game, but Jagex have kept up with technology development and improved the game over time to keep up with the evolution of video games. Since its origins as a simple browser game, Runescape has seen improved graphics and countless new additions to the world and activities.

Gielenor

Runescape takes place in the world of Gielenor, a medieval fantasy realm consisting of different regions, kingdoms, and cities. Gielenor was originally created by great beings called the Elder Gods, but as other Gods discovered the realm, many sought to ravage or claim it for themselves. It is the player's job as the World Guardian to ensure the safety of Gielenor by fighting monsters, slaying bosses, and becoming stronger. Each region of Gielenor offers different types of monsters to fight, resources to gather, and quests to improve the player's character.

Combat and Skills

There are a number of skills tied to the player's character. All skills start at level 1 but can be trained up to level 99 or 120, depending on the skill. Levelling up in these skills allows the player to do more things related to that skill. Some quests and high-level activities require certain levels in certain skills, so it is beneficial to every player to reach max level in all of them! A list of the current skills can be seen below:

  • Combat - Skills that help the player in combat
    • Attack
    • Strength
    • Defence
    • Ranged
    • Prayer
    • Magic
    • Summoning
    • Constitution
  • Gathering - Skills that involve gathering raw materials
    • Archaeology
    • Mining
    • Fishing
    • Woodcutting
    • Farming
    • Hunter
    • Divination
  • Artisan - Skills that involve using resources to create other items
    • Herblore
    • Crafting
    • Fletching
    • Smithing
    • Cooking
    • Firemaking
    • Runecrafting
    • Construction
  • Support - Skills that help the player in various ways
    • Agility
    • Thieving
    • Slayer
    • Dungoeneering

In addition to these skills, combat gear is another way of improving one's character. Runescape revolves around three style of combat; melee, ranged, and magic. Each of these combat styles has countless weapons and armor sets that can be used by the player. Some weapons and armor are stronger than others, while some gear may have niche uses where they excel. Some monsters may be weak to a certain combat style but resilient to another. To survive in the world of Gielenor, it is beneficial for a player to be a master of all three styles.
